# Sources

The framework texts analysed in this repository are not hosted here: redistribution rights vary, and a link to the source of record is better currency practice than a hosted copy that goes stale. This page carries the authoritative link for every framework the corpus tracks and every primary document the published artefacts analyse or cite, with the date each link was last checked live. Links and dates derive from the corpus dataset, `corpus_data.json`, published beside the interactive map.

## Frameworks analysed

**Anthropic Responsible Scaling Policy, v3.3** (effective 26 May 2026)
Source of record: https://www.anthropic.com/responsible-scaling-policy
Version verified current 5 July 2026. Note: the published v3.3 PDF has a pagination defect; the artefacts cite it by section, never by page.

**OpenAI Preparedness Framework, Version 2** (15 April 2025)
Announcement and landing page: https://openai.com/index/updating-our-preparedness-framework/
Document: https://cdn.openai.com/pdf/18a02b5d-6b67-4cec-ab64-68cdfbddebcd/preparedness-framework-v2.pdf
Version verified current 7 July 2026 (v2 remains operative; no v3 or addendum).
First arrival: Preparedness Framework (Beta), 18 December 2023. Document: https://cdn.openai.com/openai-preparedness-framework-beta.pdf (checked live 12 July 2026; the original announcement page no longer stands alone and redirects to OpenAI's safety hub).

**Google DeepMind Frontier Safety Framework, v3.1** (17 April 2026)
Landing page: https://deepmind.google/blog/strengthening-our-frontier-safety-framework/
Document: https://storage.googleapis.com/deepmind-media/DeepMind.com/Blog/strengthening-our-frontier-safety-framework/frontier-safety-framework_3-1.pdf
Version verified current 8 July 2026. Caution: DeepMind reused one blog URL across v3.0 and v3.1, and third-party trackers mislabel the versions as a result; the FSF's own changelog is the authority.

**ISO/IEC 42001:2023** -- Information technology -- Artificial intelligence -- Management system
Catalogue page: https://www.iso.org/standard/42001
A purchased standard; no copy is hosted or linked beyond the catalogue page. Analysed at first edition (December 2023), no amendments as of the deep-dive close (24 May 2026).

**NIST AI RMF 1.0** (AI 100-1, January 2023)
Landing page: https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework
Link checked live 12 July 2026. Current, with a revision in progress at NIST; the artefact analyses 1.0.
